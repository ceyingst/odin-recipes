Chankonabe

Description

This is a soup that sumo wrestlers eat to help gain weight. In large quantities, it supplies a lot of calories. But eaten in smaller quantities, it is an excellently balanced, high-protein meal, even for non-sumo wrestlers.

Ingredients

  • broth
  • bok choy
  • tofu
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • ginger
  • carrots
  • meatballs
  • scallions
  • mushrooms
  • other vegetables of your choosing

Steps

  1. Start making the broth (or use pre-made broth)
  2. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and sesame oil.
  3. Bring broth to a boil.
  4. Once the broth boils, turn heat down to simmer.
  5. Add additional ingredients--noting that meat items take longer to cook through, while veggies take less
  6. Serve (over rice to taste)
