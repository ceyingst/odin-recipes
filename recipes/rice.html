Perfect rice

Description

This perfect jasmine rice is a lovely complement to other recipes on this site!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ginger(optional)
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by rinsing your rice (use a wire-mesh strainer)
  2. Add rice to pot
  3. Cover rice with water (2 parts water to 1 part rice; or, stick your finger in the pot, and add enough water to hit your first knuckle)
  4. Bring pot to a boil; add salt
  5. Once it boils, turn down to simmer, and cover.
  6. Wait 20 minutes--or until rice is cooked through!
  7. Optional: add ginger and/or garlic to taste, at boil stage
